Instant Gingerbread Hot Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cocoa
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper(optional)

Directions

Step 1

Add cocoa, white sugar, brown sugar, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, salt, and cayenne to a bowl, and whisk thoroughly to combine.

step 2

Store in an airtight container until needed. Be sure to stir very thoroughly before each use.

step 3

To make hot chocolate, heat milk over medium heat until it almost starts to simmer. Whisk in 2 tablespoons of instant gingerbread hot chocolate mix per 1 cup milk.

step 4

Once everything is mixed together and dissolved, and the milk is piping hot, transfer into mugs and enjoy.

Sugar Cookie Martini

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla icing, or as needed
  • 1 teaspoon sprinkles, or as needed
  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 3 fluids ounces rum cream liqeur
  • 1 1/2 ounces vanilla vodka
  • 1/4 ounce Amaretto

Directions

step 1

Rim martini glass with icing and sprinkles; set aside.

step 2

Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; pour in rum cream, vodka, and amaretto. Cover and shake until the outside of shaker has frosted. Strain into a chilled glass.

Apple, Strawberry, and Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup vanilla almond milk
  • 1 apple peeled, cored, and cubed
  • i cup halved frozen strawberries
  • 1 frozen banana, peeled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup vanilla Greek yogurt

Directions

step 1

Place almond milk, apple, strawberries, banana, and yogurt in a blender, being sure the milk goes in first. Process until smooth, occasionally scraping down the sides of the blender with a rubber spatula, if necessary.

step 2

Pour into serving glasses and serve immediately.
